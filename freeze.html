FREEZE

FREEZE is a nervous system regulation app designed to help users move through freeze states with grounding tools, guided micro-flows, and supportive reflection.

What it does

  • Guided grounding flows for freeze states
  • Tool-based prompts (with or without a physical kit)
  • Gentle reflection questions + “I did it” progress
  • Designed for clarity, calm, and low-friction use
